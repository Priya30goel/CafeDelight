Ticket Booking FAQ

1 Can I purchase tickets at the venue?

Yes, tickets will be available for purchase at the venue. However, we recommend booking in advance as seating is limited.


2 Is there a discount for group bookings?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for group bookings of 5 or more. Contact us for more details.


3 What is the refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable. However, you can transfer your ticket to another person by contacting us at least 24 hours before the event.


4 How can I receive my ticket?

Once booked, your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email. You can either print it or show the QR code at the entrance.
